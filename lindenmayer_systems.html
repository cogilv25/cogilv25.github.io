What Is The Alphabet For The App?

F will move the turtle in the direction it's facing AND draw a line
f will move the turtle in the direction it's facing BUT NOT draw a line
+ rotate the direction the turtle is facing anti-clockwise by the angle chosen on the edit screen
- rotate the direction the turtle is facing clockwise by the angle chosen on the edit screen
[ will push the state of the turtle (position and facing direction) to the stack
] will pop the state of the turtle (position and facing direction) from the stack resetting the state to the state last pushed to the stack
